Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I create an account?

Simply click on the "Sign Up" button on the login page. Fill out the required information, and you'll be able to access the Table Tap dashboard immediately.

2. Can I manage multiple restaurants under one account?

Yes. Once logged in, you can add multiple branches or restaurant profiles, each with its own menu and QR codes.

3. How do I generate QR codes for tables?

After adding a new branch or table, simply go to the "Manage Tables" section and click "Generate QR". The system will provide a QR code for each table.

4. What subscription plans are available?

We offer monthly, quarterly, and annual plans. Check the "Subscription" tab on your dashboard for more details.

5. Where can I find more help?

If you still have questions, please visit our Help page or contact our support at support@tabletap.com.
